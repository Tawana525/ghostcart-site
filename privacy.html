Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

GhostCart ("the Extension", "we", "us", "our") is operated by Blessed Tumbela, a resident of Zimbabwe. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the GhostCart browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

a) Automatically collected data:

  • Product search queries you enter on supported retailer websites (e.g., Amazon, Best Buy, eBay).
  • URLs of product pages you visit on those sites.
  • Non-personal technical data such as browser type, extension version, and interactions with the extension interface.

b) Information you provide voluntarily:

  • Email address (if you sign up for a premium account via Lemon Squeezy or contact us directly).
  • Any feedback or messages you send to us.

c) Local storage:

  • The extension may store small amounts of data locally on your device (e.g., user preferences, temporary session identifiers). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us unless necessary for the core functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display product comparisons, deals, and relevant offers directly within your browser.
  • Generate affiliate links that allow us to earn a commission when you make a purchase through those links.
  • Process and manage premium subscriptions (via Lemon Squeezy).
  • Improve the Extension’s performance and user experience.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following third-party services that are essential for the Extension to function:

  • Amazon Associates / Impact.com
  • CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)
  • eBay Partner Network
  • Lemon Squeezy (subscription payments)
  • SerpAPI (search result retrieval)
  • Cloudflare (network infrastructure)

These services may process your data (e.g., IP address, product details) under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Extension may use local storage and similar technologies to remember your preferences. Third parties (such as affiliate networks) may set cookies in your browser when you click affiliate links. We do not control these third-party cookies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own (including the United States), where data protection laws may differ. By using the Extension, you consent to such transfers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nyashatawana483@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website or through the Extension.
